# Example Research Queries

Try these with your research agent. They're designed to test different skills.

## 1. Factual with Numbers
"Research: What are the 5 most spoken languages in the world and how many speakers does each have?"

**Tests**: Finding specific data, presenting numbers clearly.

## 2. Multiple Perspectives
"Research: What are the arguments for and against school uniforms?"

**Tests**: Presenting both sides fairly, not being biased.

## 3. Technical Explanation
"Research: How do electric car batteries work and what are the main types?"

**Tests**: Explaining technical concepts clearly for a non-expert.

## 4. Current Events
"Research: What are the biggest trends in AI right now?"

**Tests**: Finding recent information, summarizing a broad topic.

## 5. Comparison
"Research: Compare Python and JavaScript — when should you use each one?"

**Tests**: Structured comparison, practical advice, not just listing features.
