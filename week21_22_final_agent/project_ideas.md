# Final Project Ideas

Pick one of these or make up your own. The best project is one you'd actually use.

---

## 1. Study Buddy
An agent that helps you study. Skills: **Quiz Me** (generates practice questions on any topic and checks your answers) and **Explain Like I'm 5** (breaks down complex topics into simple explanations with analogies).

## 2. Journal Agent
A daily journaling companion. Skills: **Daily Prompt** (sends you a thought-provoking question each day and saves your response) and **Weekly Review** (summarizes your journal entries from the past week and spots patterns).

## 3. Sports Tracker
Keeps you updated on your favorite teams. Skills: **Game Updates** (fetches recent scores and upcoming games for teams you follow) and **Player Lookup** (finds stats and info about any player you ask about).

## 4. News Summarizer
Cuts through information overload. Skills: **Headlines** (fetches top news from sources you pick and gives 1-sentence summaries) and **Deep Dive** (takes any headline and researches it thoroughly with multiple sources).

## 5. Code Reviewer
Helps you write better code. Skills: **Review My Code** (you paste code and it finds bugs, suggests improvements, and explains issues) and **Explain This Error** (you paste an error message and it explains what went wrong and how to fix it).

## 6. Recipe Finder
A cooking assistant. Skills: **What Can I Make?** (you list ingredients you have and it suggests recipes) and **Recipe Guide** (gives step-by-step cooking instructions with timing and tips for any dish).

## 7. Workout Planner
A fitness helper. Skills: **Daily Workout** (generates a workout based on your goals, equipment, and available time) and **Exercise Guide** (explains proper form and alternatives for any exercise you ask about).

## 8. Language Practice
A language learning partner. Skills: **Conversation Practice** (has a conversation with you in your target language, correcting mistakes gently) and **Vocabulary Builder** (teaches you new words with example sentences and quizzes you later).

## 9. Music Recommender
Helps you discover music. Skills: **Recommend** (you describe a mood, activity, or artist you like and it suggests songs/albums with reasons) and **Artist Info** (researches any artist and gives you their background, best albums, and similar artists).

## 10. Daily Planner
Helps you organize your day. Skills: **Plan My Day** (you tell it your tasks and it creates a time-blocked schedule with breaks) and **Priority Helper** (you list everything on your plate and it helps you decide what's most important and what can wait).

---

## Your Own Idea

The best projects come from a real problem you have. Think about:
- What do you do every day that's tedious?
- What information do you regularly look up?
- What would you want a personal assistant to handle?

Whatever you pick, make sure it has **2 distinct skills** that work together.
